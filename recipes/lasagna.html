Lasagna Recipe

This is my favorite recipe for lasagna!

This recipe includes the famous four cheese spirit to fulfill all of your cheesy needs!

Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
How to make step by step
  1. Make the sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving
