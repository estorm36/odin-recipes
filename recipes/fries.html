Waffle Fries

This is your new go-to waffle fries recipe

These waffle fries might be better than chick fil a

Ingredients
  • potatoes
  • peanut oil
  • salt
How to make
  1. Cut the potatoes into waffle fries
  2. Add the cut fries to a bowl of ice water as they are done
  3. Fry the fries in batches until they are cooked through, then drain
  4. Increase oil heat and fry again, until they are golden brown
  5. Drain and however you like
