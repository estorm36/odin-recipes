Fluffy french toast

This is the fluffiest french toast you will ever have

French toast that will make you feel like you're in France eating toast

Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
Steps to make french toast
  1. Slowly whisk the milk into the flour, then wisk the remaining ingredients
  2. Saturate the bread slices in the batter
  3. Cook the french toast on a lighlty oiled griddle or pan until golden on both sides
